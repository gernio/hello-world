Nerd Nite St. Pete

We all know that learning is more fun when you’re drinking with friends and colleagues. Nerd Nite strives for an educational yet salacious vibe. Several presenters totally geek out for 20 minutes each while guests drink and socialize - It’s like the Discovery Channel – with cocktails! Drink, learn, connect, and talk about fun, interesting stuff!
